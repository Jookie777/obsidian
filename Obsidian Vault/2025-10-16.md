怎么设置chrome new tab to the right
- https://superuser.com/questions/1103354/keyboard-shortcut-to-open-new-tab-next-to-current-tab-in-google-chrome
- “**System Preferences > Keyboard > Shortcuts**. In the left tab, select **App Shortcuts**. Then click the **+** sign, and type "New Tab to the Right" (match capitalization)”


Iterm2发神经，没法option左 / option右
-   The proper solution is to use iTerm2's built-in preset:

  1. Preferences → Profiles → Keys → Key Mappings tab
  2. Click "Presets..." at the bottom
  3. Select "Natural Text Editing"
